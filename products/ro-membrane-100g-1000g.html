The membrane is the heart of any RO system. KomiBright systems use Canature membranes: the CN-1812-100G for C25R-series machines and the high-flow CN-3812-1000G for the C100R direct-flow system.

Thanks to the automatic flushing design of KomiBright housings, membranes reach their full service life — replace once a year along with the compound filter, no tools required.
