Far from the city, every water source is a question mark. The KB-A95R answers it with true reverse osmosis: 0.0001 μm filtration that removes sediment, microorganisms, bacteria and viruses from tap water, rivers and lakes — with zero electricity.

The compact chassis combines an outdoor filtration system with a genuinely portable format. A pre-PP filter handles rough source water before the compound filter and membrane take over. Camping deep in the mountains or touring by RV, clean drinking water stops being a logistics problem.
