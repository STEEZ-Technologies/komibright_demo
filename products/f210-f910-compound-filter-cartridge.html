Specifications

ModelsF-210 / F-910
StructurePleated PP + carbon block
FunctionSediment, chlorine, odour, VOC removal
ReplacementEvery 12 months

Conventional RO machines stack two or three separate pre-filter cartridges. KomiBright's super compound filter does the same work in one: a pleated PP layer captures particles and sediment, and a precision activated carbon block removes residual chlorine, odour and volatile organics.

The F-210 serves C25R-series systems; the larger F-910 serves the C100R direct-flow platform. Both are replaced once a year together with the membrane.
